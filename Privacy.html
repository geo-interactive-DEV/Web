Geo Interactive Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Geo Interactive (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle the collection, use, and protection of your data when you visit our website or apply to join our team.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Application details: Resumes, portfolios, skills, previous experience
  • Usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site
  • Cookies: Small text files to remember your preferences or track visits
  • Communication records: Messages you send via email or contact forms

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries or applications
  • To manage and process team member applications
  • To maintain internal team records and contact lists
  • To analyze traffic and improve website performance
  • To comply with legal or safety requirements

4. Applying to Join Geo Interactive

When you apply to join our team, we collect any information you submit including your name, email, portfolio links, and other relevant details. This data is only accessed by core team members and is used strictly for reviewing your application.

If accepted, your information will be retained securely as part of our internal records. If not accepted, your data will be deleted within 60 days unless we receive explicit permission to keep it on file for future opportunities.

We will never sell, share, or publicize your application details without your consent.

5. Communications

By contacting us or applying, you consent to receive relevant communications from us related to your inquiry or application. You may opt out of non-essential updates at any time.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies for basic functionality (such as remembering page states or preferences). We may also use anonymous analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand how users interact with our content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as form processors, cloud storage, or analytics tools. These services are selected carefully and are compliant with modern data protection standards (e.g., GDPR).

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. Access to sensitive data is limited to staff who require it for specific operational purposes.

9. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask for corrections if your information is inaccurate or outdated
  • Request deletion of your data under applicable laws
  • Withdraw consent to processing your data, where applicable

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. You are encouraged to review the policy occasionally to stay informed.

11. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@geointeractive.online
  • Website: https://geointeractive.online
