Geo Interactive Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Geo Interactive ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share your personal data when you interact with our services or website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Contact and emergency details for onboarding purposes
  • Browser and device data (IP address, type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, interactions)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to inquiries and provide services
  • To manage team applications and internal communication
  • To improve our website and services
  • To ensure legal and safety compliance

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored and only accessible by authorized personnel. We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties unless required by law or essential to providing our services (e.g., payment processing).

6. Your Rights

  • You can request access to the data we hold about you
  • You may request corrections or deletion of your personal data
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time

7. Cookies

We use basic cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

8. Apply to Join Geo Interactive

If you submit an application to join Geo Interactive, we will collect the information you provide for review and communication. This includes your name, contact details, and any documents or links you share (e.g., portfolios).

All application data is confidential and only reviewed by our core team. If you're accepted, your information will be used for onboarding purposes. If not, your data will be securely deleted within 60 days.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@geointeractive.online
  • Website: https://geointeractive.online/
