Geo Studios Collaboration Program

Last Updated: June 2025

We are currently NOT ACCEPTING new applications.

🌟 Purpose & Vision

The Geo Studios Collaboration Program connects passionate creatives, developers, and thinkers with our team on projects. It’s a chance to gain experience, grow skills, and contribute to real-world work.

🧾 Program Goals

  • Support skill development and creative experience
  • Open doors for future partnerships
  • Encourage innovation from our community
  • Promote teamwork in a safe, inclusive space

🤝 What We Offer

  • Access to real projects and builds
  • Credit in published work
  • Mentorship from experienced developers
  • Potential for future partnership invitations

🔎 How It Works

📝 Application Process

Apply via Discord or our website form. We aim to reply in 3–7 days.

📋 What to Include

  • Username / Display Name
  • Age (13+)
  • Your skills (coding, art, writing, etc.)
  • Portfolio or examples of work
  • Why you want to collaborate
  • How often you’re available

🧩 Collaboration Areas

  • Creative & Design: pixel art, concept art, UI, animation
  • Development: Lua, JS, Python, Unity, Godot
  • Audio: music, voice, sound effects
  • Story: lore, dialogue, quest writing
  • Testing: bug finding, suggestion feedback

🧑‍⚖️ Rules & Conduct

  • Be respectful and professional
  • Only submit original work
  • Keep project info confidential
  • Stay active and communicate absences
  • Follow our community guidelines

📉 Strike System

StrikesConsequence
1Warning
2Temporary removal from project
3Permanent removal from program

🚪 Exiting the Program

You can leave at any time by notifying a team member. We reserve the right to remove collaborators who are inactive or violate guidelines.

🏅 Advancement Opportunities

  • Access to beta tests and internal builds
  • Public recognition and credits
  • Invitation to our partner program

📚 FAQ

Do I get paid? This is a volunteer program. Paid roles will not be offered in the future.

Do I own my work? Anything submitted becomes part of Geo Studios. You’ll be credited but not retain IP rights.

Can I use the work in my portfolio? Yes, with attribution, after it's released publicly.
