Overview

The world appears normal to the average person. But behind the scenes, a clandestine organization known as The Veil Directive (TVD) safeguards humanity from threats that defy natural law — artifacts, creatures, locations, and events known as "Veiled Phenomena."

Seal the breach. Study the unknown. Shield the world.

The Organization

Founded in 1931 following the global event codenamed The Whisperstorm, The Veil Directive operates from shifting headquarters every 13 years to avoid detection. Its structure includes divisions focused on containment, research, armed response, and memory alteration, governed by The Overseers—five masked figures with ultimate authority.

Featured Veiled Phenomena

VP-008: The Smiling Fog

An atmospheric anomaly that whispers the names of loved ones. Victims vanish after entering its thick fog.

VP-107: Mr. Forget

A humanoid who cannot be remembered after contact. Used by TVD for covert messaging.

VP-382: The Wound That Dreams

A spatial anomaly beneath an abandoned asylum, leading to a dream dimension filled with dangerous entities.

VP-182: The Mirror Sisters

Two humanoid anomalies sharing an unexplained bond and granted unique freedoms within TVD.
