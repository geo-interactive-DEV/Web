World Concept: The Veil Directive

The world appears normal to the average person. But behind the scenes, a clandestine organization known as The Veil Directive (TVD) safeguards humanity from threats that defy natural law: artifacts, creatures, locations, and events known as "Veiled Phenomena."

"Seal the breach. Study the unknown. Shield the world."

The Organization

The Veil Directive (TVD)
Founded: 1931, after a global event codenamed The Whisperstorm.

Headquarters: Shifts locations every 13 years to avoid detection.

Structure

  • Containment Division: Secures and monitors phenomena.
  • Research Division: Studies phenomena to extract knowledge.
  • Null Response Teams: Armed units for high-threat incidents.
  • Memory Division: Wipes or alters memories of witnesses.
  • The Overseers (Council V): Five masked figures who govern TVD.

Sample Veiled Phenomena

VP-008: The Smiling Fog

Type: Atmospheric anomaly

Appearance: Thick fog that whispers the names of loved ones.

Effect: Victims are lured into it and vanish, never found.

Containment: A 10km no-entry zone established in northern Scotland, guarded under the guise of military training grounds.

VP-107: Mr. Forget

Type: Humanoid anomaly

Description: Appears as a normal man. Cannot be remembered after direct contact.

Effect: Anyone who talks to him forgets his existence after 30 seconds.

Use: Employed by TVD for covert messaging. He voluntarily works for them, but no one remembers why.

VP-382: The Wound That Dreams

Type: Spatial anomaly

Location: Beneath an abandoned asylum in Italy.

Effect: A hole that leads to a dream dimension. Entities within try to escape into our world.

Containment: Surrounding structure sealed with reinforced dream-barriers developed by the Research Division.

Security Classifications

Class Theta– Harmless or understood.

Class Omicron – Potentially dangerous, but stable.

Class Delta – Active threat to public safety.

Class Epsilon – Uncontainable, reality-altering, or godlike.

Notable Characters

  • Dr. Keira Voss – Head of Experimental Anomalies. Allegedly has modified her own memories dozens of times.
  • Agent Locke – Lead Null Response commander. Rumored to have survived direct exposure to VP-001.
  • The Oracle – A child anomaly who speaks only in future-tense and never lies.

Additional Elements

  • The Bleed: A mysterious effect where parts of reality begin to "bleed" into other dimensions.
  • Public Front: TVD operates publicly as “The Veil Foundation,” a disaster relief NGO.
  • Rival Groups: Rogue scientists, cults worshipping anomalies, and a group called “The Unshackled” trying to expose the truth.
