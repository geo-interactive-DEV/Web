Choose Your Training Program

We offer two levels of training to help you learn how to better support mental health in your community. Whether you're just starting or you're ready to advance your knowledge, we have a program for you!

MHS Training Program

The MHS Training Program is perfect for individuals who are new to mental health awareness. This program will help you understand the basics of mental health, how to identify common challenges, and how to support those who may be struggling.

Training Details:

  • Module 1: Introduction to Mental Health
  • Module 2: Recognizing Common Mental Health Challenges
  • Module 3: The Role of a Supporter
  • Module 4: Providing Support and Setting Boundaries
  • Module 5: Self-Care for Supporters
  • Module 6: Real-World Case Scenarios

This training is ideal for those who want to gain foundational knowledge to support others and make a difference in their community.

Advanced MHS Training Program

The Advanced MHS Training Program is designed for those who already have a basic understanding of mental health and want to dive deeper. This program covers advanced topics, including handling complex cases and learning more effective strategies to provide support.

Training Details:

  • Module 1: Advanced Mental Health Topics
  • Module 2: Crisis Intervention and Risk Assessment
  • Module 3: Supporting Individuals with Severe Mental Health Challenges
  • Module 4: Advanced Communication Techniques
  • Module 5: Professional Boundaries and Ethical Considerations
  • Module 6: Mental Health Advocacy and Resources

The Advanced MHS Training Program is ideal for those looking to expand their understanding of mental health support, work in professional settings, or take on a leadership role in their community.
