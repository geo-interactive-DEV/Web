The Organization

Founded in 1931 after the catastrophic global event known as The Whisperstorm, The Veil Directive (TVD) operates globally in the shadows to protect humanity.

Headquarters

The Directive’s headquarters relocates every 13 years to maintain secrecy and evade detection, employing advanced concealment technologies and misinformation campaigns.

Structure

  • Containment Division: Responsible for securing and monitoring Veiled Phenomena worldwide.
  • Research Division: Investigates phenomena to extract knowledge, develop containment methods, and innovate technology.
  • Null Response Teams: Armed tactical units deployed during high-threat incidents requiring direct intervention.
  • Memory Division: Manages memory alteration and wipes for witnesses and personnel exposed to anomalies.

The Overseers (Council V)

The Council V are five enigmatic masked figures governing TVD’s strategic decisions, operating from the shadows to maintain balance and control.
