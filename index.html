About Us

Geo Interactive is a UK-based creative studio developing original games, stories, and interactive experiences. From innovative card games to haunting fiction, we bring imagination to life.

Our Projects

  • PAX-56 – A psychological horror novel about a parasitic entity that distorts perception, identity, and truth.
  • Hearthville – A cozy open-world life sim where players build relationships, farm, and shape a living town. Currently in development.
  • Shape Shifter – A dynamic platformer with level editing, traps, and morphing mechanics. Coming soon!
  • Gmon TCG – A creature-collecting trading card game featuring colorful pixel art and original creatures like Emberpaw and Leafleap.
  • UKRP (Roblox) – A realistic UK roleplay game set in Dover, featuring custom jobs, laws, and licenses.
