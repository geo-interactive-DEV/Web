Field Protocols

  • Agents are to maintain absolute secrecy at all times.
  • Phenomena containment takes precedence over property damage.
  • Memory alteration procedures must be approved by the Memory Division lead.

Research Ethics

  • All experiments on live phenomena must minimize harm to human subjects.
  • Unauthorized use of phenomena artifacts is strictly prohibited.

Security Levels

  • Level 1 - General awareness (field agents, basic clearance)
  • Level 2 - Containment protocols (specialist teams, mid-level clearance)
  • Level 3 - Research & development (research staff, high clearance)
  • Level 4 - Council & Oversight (executive members only)
