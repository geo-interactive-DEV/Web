Welcome to ThriveWell

ThriveWell is a mental health support program designed to provide awareness, education, and tools for individuals who want to make a positive impact on others' mental well-being.

Our goal is to empower supporters to be compassionate, empathetic, and equipped with the right tools to provide support in times of need.

Training Objectives

  • Raise awareness about mental health and its importance.
  • Learn how to recognize warning signs and offer support.
  • Understand the role of a supporter and maintain healthy boundaries.
  • Provide practical tools for supporting others in mental health struggles.
  • Understand the importance of self-care for supporters.
