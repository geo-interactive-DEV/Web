Notable Characters

Dr. Keira Voss

Position: Head of Experimental Anomalies

Dr. Voss is renowned for her pioneering research into experimental containment techniques and her controversial personal memory modifications. Her work often blurs ethical boundaries, but has yielded breakthrough results in controlling unstable phenomena.

Agent Locke

Role: Lead Null Response Commander

A seasoned field operative with multiple successful interventions, Agent Locke is rumored to have survived direct exposure to VP-001. Known for his unshakable calm in crisis and tactical brilliance, he commands the Directive’s most dangerous containment missions.

The Oracle

Type: Child Anomaly

The Oracle is an enigmatic child who communicates exclusively in future tense and is famously incapable of lying. Her cryptic prophecies and guidance have shaped many of the Directive’s critical decisions.

Director Alistair Crane

Position: Current Director of The Veil Directive

Known for his strategic mind and secretive nature, Director Crane oversees all operations and liaises with the Council V. His close connection to VP-182 (The Mirror Sisters) adds an air of mystery to his leadership.

Professor Elias Mendel

Role: Senior Researcher, Temporal Anomalies Division

Specializing in anomalies affecting time and causality, Professor Mendel has developed several devices used in temporal containment. His obsession with preventing paradoxes often puts him at odds with other divisions.
