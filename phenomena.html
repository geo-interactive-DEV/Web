Veiled Phenomena

The Veil Directive classifies and contains numerous anomalies, referred to as Veiled Phenomena (VP). These range from atmospheric disturbances to entities defying human understanding.

VP-008: The Smiling Fog

Type: Atmospheric Anomaly

A thick, whispering fog that lures victims to their disappearance in northern Scotland.

VP-107: Mr. Forget

Type: Humanoid Anomaly

A man who cannot be remembered after interaction. Used for covert messaging.

VP-382: The Wound That Dreams

Type: Spatial Anomaly

A hole beneath an abandoned asylum in Italy leading to a dangerous dream dimension.

VP-182: The Mirror Sisters

Type: Humanoid Anomalies (Two Individuals)

Two children with a mysterious bond and anomalous pupil dilation, closely connected to the Director.
